About

Prodigy Infotech is an innovative platform dedicated to providing free internships for students and unemployed individuals. This initiative aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world experience, offering participants a unique opportunity to enhance their skills and boost their employability. The platform hosts a variety of internship programs across different domains, ensuring that there's something for everyone, regardless of their field of study or career interests.

Participants are required to complete specific tasks and projects during their internship, designed to simulate real-world challenges and foster practical learning. These tasks not only help in building a strong portfolio but also in gaining hands-on experience that is highly valued by employers.
